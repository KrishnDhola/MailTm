What is MailTm?

MailTm is an open-source email service that gives you temporary, disposable inboxes to protect your privacy online. No sign-up required.

Why Use It?

  • ✔️ Keep your real email safe
  • ✔️ Avoid spam
  • ✔️ Use one-time emails for sign-ups
  • ✔️ 100% free and open source
